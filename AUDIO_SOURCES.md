# External Audio Sources

Raw WAV files are intentionally not stored in git.

- Ambience: Gregor Quendel, Free General Ambience Sounds
  - https://gregorquendel.bandcamp.com/album/free-general-ambience-sounds
  - https://opengameart.org/content/free-general-ambience-sounds
- Footsteps: Footsteps, Unity Asset Store
  - https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/audio/sound-fx/footsteps-238257

HUD WAV sounds are locally generated project assets and are also ignored as raw audio.
